THE HISTORIC HEART OF LIMA TOUR

Our adverture starts by visiting ‘Plaza de Armas’, see the famous Cathedral of Lima, the Presidential Palace and take in the beauty of the spanish architecture. ‘Peruvian Cuisine’, we are going to take some time out from the tour to have lunch*, indulging your tastebuds with traditional Peruvian food and drink, the reason why you came to Peru: to enjoy the most desirable culinary experience imaginable in the centre of Lima.Then we are going to visit the ‘Church of San Francisco / Catacombs’, Not for the squeamish, but an undeniably unforgettable experience. ‘The Chocolate Museum’, enjoy handcrafted Peruvian chocolate and try out your own chocolate-making skills. We are ending with some cheers at the ‘Pisco Museum’, relax with Peru’s most famous alcoholic beverage: Pisco Sour, what better way to learn about the history of this iconic drink than by experiencing it yourself.
